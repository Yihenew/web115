KAFFA COFFEE SHOP

Your day begins after a cup of coffee



Brand Guide

KAFFA COFFEE is a future coffee shop which will be located in the Addis Ababa. My mother is very famous in making cultural coffee and she always has thought to have a nice coffee shop. Now she is on the verge of retiring so it's time to start a new challenge. The idea comes from my mom but my father also part of it so it is a family business. after the shop open it will serve with a variety of coffee as well as different kind of delicious dessert cakes
